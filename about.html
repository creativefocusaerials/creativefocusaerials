the Lens

Meet the Founder

Levi Forsbloom — Professional Drone Pilot, Filmmaker, and Avid Golfer.

With over 10 years of experience in film, television, and aerial media production, I've spent my career studying how to tell visual stories that move people.

But my real passion lies at the intersection of cinema and golf. I've filmed dozens of courses — from municipal tracks to exclusive private clubs — and I understand what makes each hole unique. The way morning light catches a dew-covered fairway. The drama of a peninsula green surrounded by water. The strategic beauty of a well-placed bunker.

As a fully licensed and insured drone pilot, I bring professional-grade equipment, meticulous flight planning, and an eye for cinematic composition to every project. When you work with Creative Focus Aerials, you're not just hiring a drone operator — you're hiring a filmmaker who speaks golf.

Our Approach

How We Capture Your Course

Every flight is planned with purpose. We combine technical precision, creative vision, and a deep understanding of golf to deliver content that performs.

Precision Planning

Before we ever take off, we study your course layout, identify the best angles, and plan flight paths that capture every hole at its most compelling.

Creative Vision

We don't just fly overhead. We craft cinematic sequences with smooth movements, dynamic transitions, and storytelling that reveals each hole's character.

Golf Strategy Understanding

We understand dogleg lefts, risk-reward carries, and pin positions. Our footage communicates the strategic beauty that makes golfers want to play your course.
