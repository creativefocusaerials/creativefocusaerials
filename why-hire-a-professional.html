Cinematic Quality

Why Smooth Cinematic Flyovers Matter

A cinematic hole flyover does what no ground photo can: it reveals the full architecture of a hole, builds anticipation, and gives golfers a reason to book.

When a player watches a smooth, sweeping flyover of your signature hole, they don't just see a golf hole — they feel the experience. Amateur drone footage with jerky movements and awkward angles does the opposite. It makes your great course look average.

Courses with professional flyover videos report up to 40% more website engagement and longer visitor session times.

Safety First

Safety, Licensing & Reliability

Flying a drone commercially over a golf course isn't as simple as buying one off the shelf. Federal law requires FAA Part 107 certification, and your course requires proper insurance coverage to protect against liability.

When you hire Creative Focus Aerials, you get a fully licensed, insured, and experienced pilot who understands airspace regulations, safe operating procedures, and the unique challenges of filming over golf courses with active play.

Every flight is planned, legal, and covered by comprehensive liability insurance.

First Impressions

First Impressions Matter in Golf Marketing

Before a golfer ever drives through your entrance, they've already formed an opinion about your course based on what they've seen online. Your website, your social media, your Google listing — these are your first tee.

If those first impressions are powered by shaky smartphone clips or grainy photos, you're telling potential players that your course doesn't take quality seriously. Professional drone content sends the opposite message: this is a course that values excellence.

88% of golfers research courses online before booking. Make sure your first impression reflects the quality of your course.
